About Me

I'm a front-end developer at The Whisky Exchange, where I build and maintain the digital experiences behind one of the UK's leading spirits retailers. My path into web development came through a career change — after years working as a Data Analyst, I took the leap and completed an intensive four-month Front End Web Development Bootcamp.

That analytical background gives me a different perspective on problem-solving: I think in systems, care about performance, and always want to understand the "why" behind the code. I love turning designs into polished, responsive interfaces and I'm constantly pushing myself to learn more and build better.
